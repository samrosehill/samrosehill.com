## What it is

A brand and product information site for Plain Toothpaste, a minimalist Australian fluoride toothpaste sold through [ethical.dental](https://ethical.dental). The site presents patient-facing ingredient and formulation details. All profits from sales go to the [Coffs Coast Wildlife Sanctuary](https://coffscoastwildlifesanctuary.com.au/).
